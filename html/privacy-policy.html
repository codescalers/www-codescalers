3. Data Processing in Connection with the Website

3.1 Visiting the Website

When you visit the Website, technical information may be automatically collected and stored by our hosting provider in server log files. This information is not assigned to specific individuals and is not combined with other data sources.

The collected technical data may include:

  • Anonymized IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL
  • Date and time of access
  • Requested pages or resources

This processing is necessary to ensure the security, stability, and proper functioning of the Website and constitutes our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Server log data is retained only for as long as technically necessary and is deleted automatically.

3.2 Cookies

The Website may use cookies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

Cookies may be used to:

  • Ensure basic website functionality
  • Improve performance and security
  • Collect anonymized usage statistics (where enabled)

You may prevent or restrict cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

Non-essential cookies, including analytics cookies, are only set after you have provided consent via a cookie banner, where required by law.

3.3 Analytics (Matomo)

The Website may use Matomo Analytics, an open-source web analytics platform.

Where enabled, Matomo collects anonymized usage data such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the Website
  • Referring pages
  • Device and browser type
  • Approximate geographic location (anonymized)

IP addresses are anonymized before storage using IP anonymization.

Analytics data is used solely to understand how the Website is used and to improve content and performance. We do not use this data to identify individual users.

You may opt out of Matomo tracking at any time via the cookie consent mechanism or browser settings.

3.4 Contact via Email

The Website does not provide a contact form.

If you voluntarily contact us via email, we will process the information you provide (such as your name, email address, and message) solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) or your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
